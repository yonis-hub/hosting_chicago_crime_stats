Crimes vs. Arrests

The visual above shows the breakdown of crimes reported in Chicago and whether or not the incident resulted in an arrest. 12% of all violent crimes resulted in an arrest. Homicide crimes had the highest percentage of arrests made while making up the smallest percentage of overall crime.
